When I'm not working with a computer, you can find me in the woods and mountains, on the river, or listening to music in between.

Skills:

Python, PostgreSQL/pgAdmin, PostGIS, Docker, Git/GitHub, Jira, QGIS
SQL Server, ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Desktop, ArcGIS Server, ArcGIS Online, Portal for ArcGIS, Web AppBuilder for ArcGIS, Survey123 for ArcGIS, ArcGIS API for JavaScript

Past Projects:


Public Utility Chemical Data Management System

Managed the design and implementation of a data management system for tracking chemical inventories, transportation, and order fulfillment. Forms were developed using Survey123 for ArcGIS and deployed from the client's ArcGIS Online platform. Users can now submit electronic forms to manage valuable assets with improved accuracy and accountability.


Public Utility Sewer District Mapping

Responsible for managing the task of converting outdated sewer system records to a modern GIS. This involved scanning, georeferencing, and digitizing engineering plans to develop a GIS database of district boundaries, sewer system subdivisions, and sewer network infrastructure.


Mitigation Banking Projects

Responsible for the design and implementation of data collection and management workflows, enterprise database management, preparation of mapping deliverables, and development of web applications for data sharing.


PA Pipeline Projects

Function as database administrator of a versioned enterprise database in support of permitting and route development efforts for multiple pipeline projects. Responsibilities include management of ecological and site development data, producing permit figures, preparing and processing GPS data, preparing field maps, producing client data deliverables, and spatial analysis for environmental impact assessments.


OH & WV Pipeline Projects

Responsible for managing spatial data in support of permitting and route development efforts. Responsibilities included management of ecological data, producing permit map figures, preparing and processing GPS data, and landcover analysis for threatened and endangered species surveys.


Pipeline Rare Plant Surveys

Responsible for managing spatial data and cartographic deliverables in support of multiple rare plant surveys along proposed pipelines and access roads.


Substation Site Development

Responsible for managing spatial data in support of permitting. Responsibilities included management of ecological data, producing permit map figures, preparing and processing GPS data, and preparing client data deliverables.


Impoundment Site Assessments

Responsible for data management and computer cartography in support of soil sampling reporting, water quality reporting, soil remediation planning, and impoundment closure planning.


AST Compliance Assessments

Responsible for developing a spatial analysis capable of producing a health and safety database and mapping products for more than 750 Above-ground Storage Tank (AST) sites.
